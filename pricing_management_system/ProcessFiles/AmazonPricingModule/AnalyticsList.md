For analytics, the dashboard should answer **business questions**, not just show charts. For your dataset, I’d structure it like this:

## Sales Questions

1. What is the total revenue generated?
2. How many total orders were received?
3. How many total units were sold?
4. What is the average order value (AOV)?
5. What is the average selling price per unit?
6. How is revenue trending day-wise, week-wise, and month-wise?
7. Which days generate the highest sales?
8. Which months perform best?
9. Is sales growth increasing or decreasing over time?

## Transaction Questions

10. What percentage of transactions are shipments vs refunds vs cancellations?
11. How many orders were refunded?
12. How many orders were cancelled?
13. What is the refund rate?
14. Which transaction types contribute most to revenue loss?
15. Are replacement orders increasing?

## Product Questions

16. Which SKUs generate the highest revenue?
17. Which SKUs sell the most quantity?
18. Which ASINs are top performers?
19. Which products are slow-moving?
20. Which products have high refunds?
21. Which products have low sales but high tax?
22. Which products contribute 80% of sales?
23. Which HSN categories generate the most revenue?

## Customer Geography Questions

24. Which states place the most orders?
25. Which cities generate highest revenue?
26. Which states buy the highest quantity?
27. Which states have high refund rates?
28. Which postal codes have maximum sales?
29. Which regions are growing fastest?
30. Which locations have poor performance?

## Shipping Questions

31. Where are shipments originating from?
32. Which Ship_From cities handle most orders?
33. Which Ship_From states generate maximum shipments?
34. What are the most common shipping routes?
35. Which routes have high refund rates?

## Warehouse Questions

36. Which warehouse processes the most orders?
37. Which warehouse generates the most revenue?
38. Which warehouse has the highest refund percentage?
39. Is workload distributed evenly across warehouses?
40. Which warehouse is underutilized?

## Fulfillment Questions

41. Which fulfillment channel performs best?
42. Which fulfillment channel generates maximum revenue?
43. Which fulfillment channel has high returns?
44. Which fulfillment channel handles most volume?
45. Which fulfillment model is most efficient?

## Tax Questions

46. How much tax was collected?
47. What is the effective tax percentage?
48. Which states generate highest tax?
49. Which products contribute highest tax?
50. Is tax collection changing over time?

## Order Quality Questions

51. Are customers ordering larger quantities over time?
52. What is average quantity per order?
53. Are duplicate invoice numbers present?
54. Are duplicate order IDs present?
55. Are there unusual transaction patterns?

## Operational Questions

56. Which SKU should be restocked first?
57. Which products should be discontinued?
58. Which states need inventory allocation?
59. Which warehouse needs optimization?
60. Which fulfillment process is causing losses?

## Executive Questions

61. What are the top business drivers?
62. Where is revenue leaking?
63. What contributes most to returns?
64. Which products should receive more inventory?
65. Which products should receive more marketing focus?
66. What changed compared to last month?
67. What are the biggest business risks?
